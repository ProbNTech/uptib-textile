## **_UK–Pakistan Textile Trade Performance (2025)_**

The United Kingdom continues to be one of Pakistan’s most important export destinations, particularly for textile and apparel products.

According to 2025 international trade statistics, Pakistan’s exports to the United Kingdom reached approximately **USD 2.09 Billion**, with textile and apparel products representing the overwhelming majority of exports.

Combined textile-related exports exceeded **USD 1.7 Billion**, demonstrating the strong position of Pakistani textile manufacturers within the UK market.

### UK Imports from Pakistan (2025)

The United Kingdom imported approximately **USD 2.27 Billion** worth of goods from Pakistan during 2025, with textiles accounting for the largest share.

Major import categories included:

- Knitwear and apparel: **USD 707.38 Million**

- Home textiles, bed linen, towels and textile articles: **USD 647.76 Million**

- Woven apparel: **USD 366.98 Million**

- Cotton products: **USD 40.16 Million**

Within the home textile sector alone, UK imports of Pakistani bed linen, table linen, kitchen linen and bath products exceeded **USD 597 Million** during 2025, highlighting Pakistan’s strong competitiveness in this category.

### Official UK Government Trade Statistics

UK Government trade data for the twelve months ending June 2025 shows:

- Clothing imports from Pakistan valued at approximately **£720 Million**

- Textile fabric imports from Pakistan valued at approximately **£520 Million**

Together, clothing and textile fabrics represented more than **77% of all UK goods imports from Pakistan**, confirming textiles as the backbone of bilateral trade between both countries.
