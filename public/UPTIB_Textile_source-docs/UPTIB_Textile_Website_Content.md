# 1. How this website works (the plan)

## 1.1 The structure, in one idea

The site is built around **two simple hubs**: **Products** — the textiles we trade — and **Services** — what we do. A visitor either wants to _look at textiles_ or wants _something done_. That is the whole mental model — nothing to decode, nothing to mix up.

| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

Everything else — Home, About, the supplier Directory and Contact — supports those two menus.

## 1.5 Brand voice & writing rules

- **Clear over clever. **Short sentences, plain words — readable on first pass by a buyer in Britain and an exporter in Faisalabad alike.

- **Benefit first. **Say what the visitor gets before how we do it.

- **One idea per block. **Each section makes a single point, then moves on.

- **Always end with a next step. **Every page closes with a clear action.

## 1.6 Design & layout principles (the “no clutter” brief)

- **Lots of white space **and generous gaps between sections.

- **Two colours only, used sparingly: **brand teal (#1BB0CE) for action, deep navy for headings; the rest is white and soft grey.

- **Consistent templates. **Every Product page looks the same as the others; every Service page looks the same as the others. Familiar layout = clarity.

- **Cards for choices, prose for explanation; **real, consistently-treated photography — never random stock.

- **Mobile first — **the drop-down menus must work cleanly on a phone.

## 1.7 How this fits with the main UPTIB site

The main site ([ukpaktrade.org.uk](https://ukpaktrade.org.uk)) is the broad trade-and-investment board. This is the focused **textile vertical** inside it — keeping the UPTIB brand, logo, colours, offices and contact details, but presenting textiles clearly through Products and Services.

**SERVICES · BUYING HOUSE (OUTSOURCING)**

** Request a quote → ** ** Send us your brief **

**How it works:**

- **Brief & quote. **We capture your requirement in full — product, fabric, thread count/GSM, sizes, quantities, certifications, target price, packaging and delivery date.

- **Supplier match. **We match your brief to the right manufacturer(s) from our vetted network.

- **Sampling & approval. **We arrange samples, manage revisions, and secure your written approval before any bulk commitment.

- **Price & order. **We negotiate competitive pricing without compromising quality, and place the order.

- **Production monitoring. **We inspect at roughly 40–50% completion — catching issues while there’s still time to fix them.

- **Final quality audit. **We carry out pre-shipment inspection to your agreed AQL standard. Nothing ships until it passes.

- **Documentation & delivery. **We handle the paperwork and coordinate freight through to UK delivery (see Logistics).
