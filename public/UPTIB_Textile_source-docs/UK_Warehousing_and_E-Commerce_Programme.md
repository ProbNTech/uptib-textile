## **UK Warehousing, E-Commerce & Amazon Market Access Programme for Pakistani Textile Exporters**

_"Made in Pakistan – Sold Across Britain **&** Europe."_

# **Our Vision**

Our objective is to establish a dedicated UK-Pakistan Textile Marketplace where Pakistani manufacturers can showcase, warehouse, market and sell products directly within the United Kingdom and European markets.

This initiative will help transform Pakistani exporters from suppliers into international brand owners while strengthening bilateral trade and investment between the United Kingdom/Europe and Pakistan.

# **Benefits for Pakistani Exporters**

**_"From Factory in Pakistan to Consumer in the UK"_**

Instead of selling a container once to an importer, exporters can:

• Build their own brand in UK & Europe

• Sell directly to UK consumers

• Increase profit margins

• Develop recurring revenue streams

• Expand into Europe in future phases

### **Executive Summary**

The UK Pakistan Trade & Investment Board (UPTIB), London, offering a dedicated UK Warehousing and E-Commerce Programme designed to help Pakistani textile manufacturers and exporters sell directly to UK consumers, retailers, wholesalers, and online marketplaces. Traditionally, Pakistani exporters rely on international buyers and importers, resulting in lower profit margins and limited brand recognition. Our program enables manufacturers to establish a direct route to the UK market through:

• UK warehousing

• Amazon UK seller account support

• Amazon FBA fulfilment

• Shopify e-commerce stores

• eBay UK

• TikTok Shop

• Etsy, and other online marketplaces

• UK distribution

• B2B buyer introductions

• Order fulfilment

• Returns management

This model allows Pakistani manufacturers to move beyond traditional export sales and build long-term brands in the UK market. UK buyers increasingly prefer next-day delivery, making UK-based warehousing essential for successful online sales.

# Our Solution

## 1. UK Market Entry

UKPTIB assists exporters with:

• UK business setup guidance

• Amazon UK seller registration

• VAT registration guidance

• Product compliance support

• Trademark and brand registration support

• Product listing preparation

## 2. UK Warehousing

Products are shipped from Pakistan to the UK and stored in our logistics partner warehouses.

Services include:

• Container receiving

• Inventory storage

• Stock management

• Product inspection

• Labelling and packaging

• Pick and pack operations

UK fulfilment centres routinely provide storage, inventory management, Amazon preparation, and direct-to-consumer dispatch services.

## 3. Amazon UK Program

UKPTIB assists manufacturers in launching products on Amazon UK.

Services include:

• Seller account setup

• Brand Store creation

• Product listing optimization

• Professional photography

• SEO optimization

• PPC advertising management

• Inventory planning

• Customer review management

Amazon FBA allows products to be stored and delivered through Amazon’s fulfilment network, significantly increasing visibility and delivery speed.

## 4. Direct E-Commerce

Exporters receive:

• Dedicated Shopify store

• UK payment gateway integration

• Digital marketing campaigns

• Google Shopping integration

• Facebook & Instagram advertising

• Email marketing campaigns

This allows manufacturers to build their own UK brands rather than relying solely on wholesale buyers.

# Revenue Model

### Warehousing Fee:

Monthly storage charges based on pallet or cubic meter usage.

### Amazon Management Fee

Percentage of monthly sales or fixed management fee.

### E-Commerce Management Fee

Store management and digital marketing services.

### Fulfilment Fee

Pick, pack and dispatch charges per order.

### Brand Development Fee

Optional branding, packaging and marketing support.
