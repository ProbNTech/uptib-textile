## UK Sportswear Market Value

According to industry market research, the UK sportswear market reached approximately US$ 6.78 Billion (£5.3 Billion) in 2025 and is projected to grow to more than US$ 9.36 Billion by 2034.

Other market estimates indicate that the broader UK sportswear and activewear sector exceeds £16.8 Billion, reflecting strong demand across fitness apparel, athleisure, teamwear, performance clothing, and sports fashion categories.

# Growth Drivers in the UK Market

The rapid growth of the UK sportswear industry is being driven by:

- Increasing health and fitness awareness

- Growth in gym memberships

- Rising participation in running, cycling, football, cricket, rugby, and fitness activities

- Expansion of e-commerce sportswear brands

- Growing demand for fashion athleisure

- Strong consumer spending on performance apparel

- Demand for sustainable and recycled-material sportswear products

Industry reports identify health-conscious lifestyles and athleisure fashion as the primary growth drivers across the UK market.
