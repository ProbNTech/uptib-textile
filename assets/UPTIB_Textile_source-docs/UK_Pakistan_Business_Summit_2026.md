**_Summary Trade and Investment statistics_**

**_ in between UK & Pakistan_**

The United Kingdom and Pakistan share a long-standing relationship rooted in history, culture, and strong people-to-people ties. Despite this, the full potential of bilateral trade and investment remains underutilized. The proposed UK–Pakistan Business Summit aims to serve as a high-impact platform to promote Pakistani exports, attract UK investment into Pakistan, and enable Pakistani businessmen to better access and understand the UK market.

The Summit will bring together policymakers, investors, trade bodies, chambers of commerce, sector leaders, and entrepreneurs from both countries. Through focused business-to-business (B2B) meetings, sector-specific discussions, and investment presentations, the event will facilitate tangible partnerships and long-term commercial relationships.

As per available info total UK-Pakistan trade in goods and services reached £5.6 billion in the four quarters to Q3 2026, a 13.5% increase from the prior year, with UK exports at £3.0 billion, primarily services.

**_Tax-free Trade between the UK and Pakistan:_**

Under the UK's Developing Countries Trading Scheme (DCTS), granting zero tariffs on about 92-94% of Pakistani goods entering the UK, significantly boosting exports like textiles and pharmaceuticals. UK’s scheme: Developing Countries Trading Scheme (DCTS), with Zero Tariffs, and this scheme allows most Pakistani products (around 92-94%) to enter the UK with no import duties (tariffs).

Pakistani Exporters can benefit from this, saving significant tariff costs, supporting its economic growth, and aiming to double bilateral trade, particularly in Agriculture, mining, textiles, IT, and pharmaceuticals

**_Total trade in goods and services_** (exports plus imports) between the UK and Pakistan was £5.6 billion in the four quarters to the end of Q3 2025, an increase of 13.5% or £661 million in current prices from the four quarters to the end of Q3 2024. In the four quarters to the end of Q3 2025, total UK exports to Pakistan amounted to £3.0 billion (an increase of 19.3% or £494 million in current prices, compared to the four quarters to the end of Q3 2024). Of all UK exports to Pakistan in the four quarters to the end of Q3 2025, £673 million (22.1%) were goods and £2.4 billion (77.9%) were services.

**_HOW TO PREPARE TO ATTEND BUSINESS SUMMIT_**

We have been hosting these events over the last decade and received plenty of feedback from participating companies and their representatives. Business Summit is a platform enabling networking, talks and new business but you need to prepare yourself for maximum results, here’re some of best practices:

**1.** **Do Your Homework**: Make a list of industries, business sectors or companies you wish to meet during the Summit. Google the market, analyze data, contact your own Embassy, Consulates, Honorary Consulates, and trade bodies. Whatever information you feel you’re needing, look for it. And most of all, who do you want to meet and why?

**2. Invite Your Customers**: Go through your own customer database or any earlier contacts you have had, or emails you received from potential new customers asking about your products or services. Maybe someone has indicated their interest in becoming your supplier or distributor? Make sure these will come and meet you at the Summit – there are plenty of rooms to meet them during the official agenda.

**3. Prepare Your Materials and Offer:** Make sure that you have materials ready with you at the Summit and keep clear what you are actually looking for. New business, more business, new partners, distributors, suppliers, agent? Clearer message, better results. And keep changing business cards and make sure you get back to everyone you met.

**4. Use Summit to Promote You and Your Company:** Summit offers a great opportunity to promote your company for new business and new customers. In addition to normal visibility there are also various opportunities for sponsoring, running your own mini exhibition or meetings, own stands or something else.

**5. And finally, the golden rule #1:** Be Active! And start it now. Start to collect details and build your own Summit package based on the things above. Meet people at the Summit, talk, change business cards, be active. Many of the Pakistani companies are multi-business corporations or family offices, something the Finnish companies used to be years ago, so talk and meet because you never know what is waiting for you behind the corner.
