## 1. PAKISTAN FIBRES (Pvt.) Ltd — "We Weave the Standard™"

**Website:** https://pakistanfibres.com/ · **Location:** Faisalabad, Pakistan · **Founded:** ~2002 (registered Pvt Ltd 2013), 15+ years

### Company overview

Pakistan Fibres is a vertically integrated B2B textile **manufacturer, bulk processor and stitcher** — explicitly _not_ an intermediary or reseller. What began as a fabric-export sourcing operation has grown into a textile hub supplying towels, bed linen, canvas and custom fabric to demanding clients across the **Middle East, North America, the Gulf and Europe**. It runs its own yarn procurement, advanced dyeing, stitching setups and logistics, markets a nationwide made-ups brand "**Dreamer's**", and supplies local giants **Gul Ahmed and Nishat**. Stated mission: "Deliver the best product at the best price — every time — no excuses." The company stresses ready stock for urgent orders and product lines refined from real end-user feedback (hotel housekeepers, hospital ward managers, laundries).

### Client types served

Hotels, resorts & boutique stays; importers & wholesalers; retail chains & department stores; cleaning-supply companies; government & institutional buyers; long-term distribution partners; hospitals & clinics; student dormitories/hostels; linen-rental businesses.

### Differentiators (Why Pakistan Fibres)

Quality (strict yarn-to-finishing controls), Price (value per dollar), Experience (15 yrs), Capacity (large-volume stitching & dyeing), Trust (proven supply to N. America/Gulf/Europe), Responsiveness (ready stock + fast custom jobs). Internal quality checklist includes random testing for shrinkage, stitching and GSM verification.

### PRODUCT LINE 1 — Towels (a core strength)

Export-grade towels engineered to survive hundreds of washes for hotels, hospitals, commercial laundries and retail. Uses **ring-spun cotton yarn** for tighter, stronger loops; partnered dyeing units run on **German technology** for shade matching, absorption and colour-fastness.

- **Quality controls per batch:** GSM testing (ordered vs actual); colour-fastness (wash, rub, bleach); shrinkage-allowance testing; absorbency rate & pile recovery; edge & stitching durability (double-lock hems).
- **Finishing options:** combed/sheared/looped finish; cam, dobby and jacquard borders; optical white, hotel white or custom dyed; pre-shrunk & pre-washed.
- **Sizes / GSM:** Face towel 30×30 cm (400–500 GSM); Hand towel 50×90 cm (450–600 GSM); Bath towel 70×140 cm (500–650 GSM); Bath sheet 100×180 cm (600–700+ GSM). Overall GSM range 400–700+. Custom sizes/constructions on request.
- **Colours:** white (standard), cream/beige, grey, navy, or custom dyed. **Branding:** embroidery, woven/printed labels, private label & packaging.
- **Export packing:** bale packing for bulk; boxed sets for retail; barcode/labeling; cartons to buyer spec with weight docs. **MOQ (export):** 500–1000 pcs per size/colour (mixing by agreement). **FOB ports:** Karachi or Port Qasim. **Lead time:** 14–25 days. Also offers bath mats and a "launch your own towel brand" / private-label programme.
