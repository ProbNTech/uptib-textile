# UK Healthcare Textile Market

The UK medical textile market is projected to reach approximately US$ 1.84 Billion and continues to grow due to increasing healthcare expenditure, hospital expansion, infection-control requirements, and demand for advanced healthcare textiles.

Healthcare and hygiene textile products are among the fastest-growing segments within the UK medical textile industry.

# UK Hospital Linen Market Size

The UK hospital linen supply and management market generated approximately US$ 716 Million in 2023 and is forecast to exceed US$ 1.32 Billion by 2030, growing at more than 9% annually.

This growth is being driven by:

- Increased hospital admissions

- Expansion of healthcare facilities

- Rising hygiene requirements

- Infection-control regulations

- Outsourcing of healthcare linen services

- Growth of private healthcare providers

# Key UK Market Trends (2026–2030)

## Infection Control & Hygiene Compliance

Healthcare buyers increasingly require:

- Antimicrobial fabrics

- High-temperature washable textiles

- Durable industrial-laundry performance

- Healthcare-certified materials

Healthcare textiles must comply with strict infection-control standards and laundering requirements.

## Sustainable Healthcare Textiles

Growing demand exists for:

- Sustainable cotton products

- Recycled textile content

- Eco-friendly manufacturing

- Low-carbon supply chains

Many NHS procurement programmes now include sustainability requirements within supplier evaluations.

## Outsourced Healthcare Procurement

Hospitals increasingly work with:

- Linen management providers

- Healthcare procurement companies

- Uniform rental providers

- National healthcare supply contractors

This creates opportunities for overseas manufacturers supplying large-scale institutional textile programmes.
